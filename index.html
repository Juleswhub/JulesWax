About Jules Wax

Based in Luxembourg, I craft visual stories across film, design, and immersive experiences. With a multidisciplinary approach spanning short films, corporate productions, UX design, and installation art, I explore the intersection of technology, narrative, and human experience.

My work has been featured at international film festivals including LuxFilmFest, FilmFestival Max Ophuls Preis, and the INDIE SHORT AWARDS Buenos Aires. I've also contributed to cultural exhibitions across Europe, including the European Capital of Culture 2023 in Eleusis, Greece.

I approach each project with curiosity and attention to detail, whether directing short films, designing user experiences, or creating immersive installations. My goal is to create work that resonates emotionally while pushing creative boundaries.
